BEIJING WU

I am a Principal Scientist in the Reagent and Assay Development at Roche Diagnostic Solutions based in Pleasanton, California.

I am a graduate student at the University of California, Berkeley, studying for a Master of Information and Data Science (expected graduation Summer 2023).

My career goal is to maximize the synergy between genomic medicine and biomedical informatics to provide patients with precisely targeted treatment recommendations. By developing the tools and quantitative methods needed to discern biological signals and elucidate patterns in large data sets, I hope to inform product strategies and guide assay and bioinformatic pipeline development.
